How Pinjemin Works

Sharing economy made simple. Earn money from items you rarely use, or rent what you need at a fraction of the cost.

The Process

Follow these simple steps to start sharing or borrowing items in your community.

1
List Your Items

Take photos of your items and create detailed listings. Set your daily rental rate, describe the item's condition, and specify availability.

2
Connect & Coordinate

Receive rental requests, chat with potential renters, and arrange pickup or delivery. Confirm dates and finalize the details through our secure platform.

3
Earn & Save

Get paid securely when you lend your items. Save money when you rent from others. Leave and receive reviews to build your reputation in the community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to list an item?

Listing items on ShareIt is completely free. We take a small service fee only when a rental is completed.

What if my item gets damaged?

Renters are responsible for returning items in the same condition. We recommend documenting the condition before
and after rental.

How do I arrange pickup and return?

You can coordinate directly with the other party through our messaging system to agree on a convenient time and
place.

What types of items can I list?

You can list most household items, tools, electronics, outdoor gear, and more. Items should be in good working condition and legal to rent.
